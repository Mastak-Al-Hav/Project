Description:

Hailing from the renowned port of Santos, this 100% Arabica is a global favorite for its flawless balance and smooth character. Cultivated at altitudes of 850–1,600m and processed naturally under the Brazilian sun, the beans develop a deep, nutty sweetness with distinct notes of toasted hazelnut and creamy caramel. Thanks to its low acidity and delicate body, Brazil Santos is the perfect choice for a daily ritual, offering a consistent and mellow cup whether brewed as a bold espresso or a gentle filter coffee. A true classic that brings the soul of Brazilian coffee culture to your home.
